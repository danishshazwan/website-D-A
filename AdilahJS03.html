About

Larry Page is the co-founder of Google and a technology entrepreneur who revolutionized the way people search for information online. He also served as the CEO of Google and Alphabet Inc., leading major innovations in artificial intelligence and digital technology.


Larry Page focuses on developing and advancing internet technologies, designing and optimizing search engine systems
